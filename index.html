About Me

My name is Daniel and I love to learn. My passion for technology came from my father. We built my first gaming desktop when I was 15 and I created my first website when I was 16. When I attended the University of Georgia, I decided to major in international affairs; it wasn't until my final year in college where I discovered I majored in the wrong field.


Upon graduating in 2015, I started my career in retail management with Kohl 's. After finishing a three month training program, I was placed in Athens, GA as an Assistant Store Manager. While at Kohl' s, I oversaw a team of over 80 part - time and full - time employees while managing departments that totaled in over $10 million in yearly revenue.I utilized the business acumen skills I learned in my training to ensure steady improvement in sales while using my leadership skills to ensure a friendly and productive atmosphere for employees and customers.


After two years at Kohl's, I realized I wanted to have a career I was passionate about. I decided to find a job in Atlanta and attend the Georgia Tech Full Stack Development course. This part-time course lasted six months and I was taught full stack JavaScript. It was in this class I discovered my passion for JavaScript and software development.
